Good Old Fashiond Pancakes

Guide to Making Pancakes

Tired of the same old oatmeal? In need of a breakfast pick me up? Actually, no matter what time of day it is, these delicious pancakes are the perfect recipe to fill your belly full of fluffy ooey gooey buttery warmth. Just follow the simple recipe below!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions

  1. Step One: In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  2. Step Two: Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
  3. Step 3: Enjoy!
